FAQ

Questions We
Hear Most

Everything you need to know about working with HYTRIX. Can't find your answer? Just reach out.

How long does a typical project take?+

Most projects are completed within 4–12 weeks depending on scope and complexity. A 5-page website typically takes 3–5 weeks, while larger web applications or full brand identities may take 8–16 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our kickoff call.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?+

We work with companies at every stage — from pre-seed startups building their first MVP to Fortune 500 companies undergoing digital transformation. Our Starter package is specifically designed for early-stage businesses with tighter budgets.

What is your payment structure?+

We typically structure payments as 40% upfront to begin work, 30% at the midpoint milestone, and 30% upon project delivery. For larger enterprise projects, custom payment schedules can be arranged. We accept bank transfer, Stripe, and major credit cards.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?+

Yes — all projects include a 30-day post-launch support period. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers that include updates, performance monitoring, security patches, and priority support. Retainers start from ₹42,000/month.

Will my website be optimised for mobile and search engines?+

Absolutely. Every website we build is fully mobile-responsive and built with Core Web Vitals and technical SEO best practices from the ground up. We aim for 95+ Lighthouse scores and ensure your site is structured for maximum organic visibility.

What technologies do you use?+

Our primary stack includes Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion for frontend. For backends we work with Node.js, Supabase, Prisma, and various headless CMS platforms like Sanity and Contentful.
